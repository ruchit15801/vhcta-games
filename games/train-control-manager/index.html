HOW TO PLAY

🚦

SIGNAL CONTROL

Tap or click any signal to toggle it between STOP (red) and GO (green). Trains will halt when they approach a red signal.

🚂

SELECT A TRAIN

Click a train to select it. Use the speed slider in the control panel below to adjust its speed. Deselect by clicking again.

🛤️

TRACK NETWORK

Trains run along colored tracks. At junctions (golden diamonds), multiple routes merge. Manage signals carefully at these crossings!

🏆

EARN POINTS

Score points every time a train safely reaches a station. Perfect timing earns combo bonuses. Reach the target score before time runs out!

💥

AVOID COLLISIONS

If two trains get too close, they crash! Game over. Use signals and speed control to keep safe distances between all trains.

🚨

EMERGENCY BRAKE

Press the EMERGENCY button or hit SPACE to instantly stop all trains. Use it when danger is detected!

⭐

20 LEVELS

Progress through 5 zones: Green Valley → Iron Ridge → Steel Canyon → Neon Heights → Quantum Rail. Each zone adds more trains, faster speeds, and complex junctions.

🎯

STAR RATING

Earn up to 3 stars per level based on your score. Score 150% of target for all 3 stars. Complete all 20 levels to become a Train Control Master!
