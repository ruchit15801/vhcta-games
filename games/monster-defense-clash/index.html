⚔️ How to Play

🎯 Select a Unit

Tap a defender card from the bottom panel. Each unit has a cost shown in ⚡ Energy.

🗺️ Place on Grid

Tap any open grid cell on the battlefield. Defenders auto-attack enemies in range!

🧟 Defend Base

Waves of monsters march towards your castle 🏰. Stop them before they destroy your base!

⚡ Energy System

Energy regenerates over time. Killing enemies gives bonus energy. Spend wisely!

💥 Use Abilities

Bomb 💥, Freeze ❄️ and Fire 🔥 buttons deal massive damage to all enemies. Use when overwhelmed!

⬆️ Upgrade Towers

Tap any placed defender to upgrade damage, speed or range. Sell for 60% energy back.

👹 Enemy Types

Goblin (fast, weak), Speeder (very fast), Troll (tanky, slow), Boss (enormous HP + wings)!

🌊 Wave System

Survive all waves to win. Difficulty increases every wave. Boss levels every 10 stages!
